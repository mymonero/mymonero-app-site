⚡ Lightweight & Efficient

We put design first and ironed out the technical details of using digital money, so you can get back to business.

There's no Monero blockchain node to run. The MyMonero server does the heavy lifting for free. Forget spending three days to scan the whole blockchain from your mobile device while you wait to pay for coffee.

Send funds from anywhere, to anywhere globally, anytime. Zero bank fees, zero processing delays, zero holds. Get your entire transfer history instantly on any device with only your seed words.

MyMonero is safe and secure. The server can't withdraw funds on your behalf because your spend key never leaves your computer.

😎 Native Privacy

We natively support Monero because of its regular security updates and because it has the strongest privacy technology.

MyMonero was founded in 2014 by Monero project steward Riccardo Spagni (aka fluffypony), developed with the assistance of the Monero Core Team, and our developers are Monero core contributors. So we take privacy and security seriously.

Our apps are all open source on GitHub.

Your app data is saved locally under strong encryption and only the "view key" is shared with the server. You can also connect to your own server under Preferences.
